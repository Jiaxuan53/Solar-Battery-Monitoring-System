⚡ BatteryGuard – Smart Solar Battery Monitoring System

🚀 Quick Start: https://battery-management2-un41.vercel.app/

📌 Introduction

Hello everyone, we are a team from UTAR Kampar, represented by Jia Xuan, together with Jazzwin, Jack, and Yihui.

Our project, BatteryGuard, is developed under the theme Predictive Maintenance for SME Resilience. It is a smart solar battery monitoring system designed to help small and medium enterprises (SMEs) improve energy reliability, reduce downtime, and optimize operational efficiency.

🌍 Problem Statement

Solar energy is becoming highly cost-effective, with studies from the University of Surrey showing that electricity generation costs can be as low as 2 pence per unit in optimal conditions.

However, SMEs still face major challenges:

- Lack of real-time battery monitoring

- Difficulty detecting battery degradation

- Risk of unexpected power failures

- Inefficient energy usage during peak tariffs

As solar adoption increases, ensuring system resilience and reliability becomes critical.

💡 Proposed Solution

BatteryGuard provides an intelligent monitoring and predictive maintenance system for solar batteries.

🔍 Key Features

- Real-Time Monitoring – Tracks charge level, voltage, temperature, and power output

- AI-Powered Alerts – Detects overheating and abnormal behavior instantly

- Predictive Maintenance

- Remaining Useful Life (RUL) estimation

- Predicted failure dates

- Health status classification

- Interactive Dashboard – Visual charts for battery health and cycle trends

- Smart Recommendations – Suggests maintenance actions before failures occur
